CircleTree

Everybody loves circular dendrograms. They give you more space in the margin to fill with annotations and flirts with the awesome Circos plots. Here's my go at it in R.

Installation

At the moment the whole project just consist of a single script that you source into R and you're good to go. If people like it might brush it up and turn it into a CRAN package, so let me know what you think!

Usage

x <- hclust(my.data)
plot(x)
plot(circletree(x))
